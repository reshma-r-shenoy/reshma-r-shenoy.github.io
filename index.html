The Company
Trudova traces its roots back to 1983 when its founder, Brian Fife, started in the insurance business.
The collective experience over the past 34 years is a compass and guides Trudova and its mission today.

Our
Mission

Our mission at Trudova is to make identifying, understanding, and purchasing an insurance product as simple, efficient, and streamlined as possible for the consumer. We are completely committed to the buying process and the consumers overall experience in it. Along w/ this commitment to the consumer experience, we are focused on delivering our insurance partners' best customers at scale.

Our
Founder


Brian Fife, the President and Founder of Trudova is a seasoned insurance veteran with over 34 years of experience in the insurance industry building sales organizations, and managing call center operations within some of the leading insurance companies in the world, most recently as the President of eFinancial prior to being acquired by Fidelity Life.
